Why Montego Bay?

Montego Bay, the parish of beaches

Montego Bay, Jamaica, is a captivating destination that attracts visitors with its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and diverse attractions. The city is renowned for its picturesque white-sand beaches, such as Doctor's Cave Beach, where visitors can bask in the sun and indulge in water activities.

Montego Bay offers a lively atmosphere with bustling markets, lively nightlife, and a rich history showcased in places like the Rose Hall Great House. The city serves as a gateway to Jamaica's natural wonders, including the Dunn's River Falls and the Martha Brae River. With its warm climate, friendly locals, and a range of activities, Montego Bay is an ideal choice for those seeking a delightful Caribbean getaway.

Beaches

My favorite beaches in Montego Bay

Doctor Cave Beach

Renowned for its clear turquoise waters and powdery white sand. The beach gained its name from a famous physician who believed in the therapeutic properties of the water.

Address

Gloucester Avenue

What I like about it

Visitors can enjoy various water activities, including swimming and snorkeling, while surrounded by the picturesque beauty of the Caribbean. The beach offers amenities such as restaurants, changing facilities, and beach rentals, making it a well-loved spot for both locals and tourists alike.

Learn more

Cornwall Beach

A pristine and serene coastal gem. This picturesque beach is characterized by its soft, white sands and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Cornwall Beach offers a tranquil escape for visitors seeking relaxation and natural beauty.

Address

2 Gloucester Avenue

What I like about it

Visitors can enjoy sunbathing, swimming, and water activities in a peaceful setting. The beach is praised for its cleanliness and is equipped with amenities such as loungers and umbrellas, providing a comfortable beach experience.

Learn more

Tropical Bliss Beach

Discover the lively charm of this beautiful Montego Bay beach, offering a perfect blend of sun, sea, and sand. For a small fee, immerse yourself in an upbeat atmosphere filled with music ranging from Reggae to Hip Hop.

Address

9 Kent Avenue

What I like about it

Enjoy dance-offs, fire-eating performances, and buffet-style dining featuring delectable options like grilled lobster and curry conch and rice. Affordable drinks, inflatable relaxation in the water.
